## Our Open Source Philosophy

We believe open source can create better tools, stronger communities, and more transparent software. TerraScale supports that model, and we already maintain some public repositories today.

## What Is Open Source Today

These TerraScale projects are currently open source:

- [TerraScale Dashboard](https://github.com/TerraScale/TerraScale.Dashboard)
- [TerraScale Documentation](https://github.com/TerraScale/TerraScale.Documentation)

## Why Not Everything Is Open Source Yet

Not every part of TerraScale is open source today. That is not because we are opposed to open source. Right now, our focus is on shipping, supporting users, and making sure anything we publish publicly is ready to maintain well. Open sourcing a project takes time, review, and ongoing care, and we want to do that responsibly.

## How This May Evolve

We review this policy regularly. As the product and team continue to grow, we may open source more parts of TerraScale where it makes sense for the project and the community.

## Contributing

If you want to contribute, start with our public repositories on GitHub. Look for open issues, propose improvements, or submit pull requests for documentation and dashboard-related changes.

- Browse our repositories on [GitHub](https://github.com/TerraScale)
- Contribute to the [Documentation repository](https://github.com/TerraScale/TerraScale.Documentation)
- Contribute to the [Dashboard repository](https://github.com/TerraScale/TerraScale.Dashboard)
