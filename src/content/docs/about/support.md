## Community Support

Join our Discord community for real-time help, product discussions, and shared advice from the TerraScale team and other users: [Join our Discord community](https://discord.gg/your-invite-link).

## Email Support

Send us a message at [support@terrascale.com](mailto:support@terrascale.com) for technical issues, account questions, and billing inquiries.

## Reporting Issues

When you report a bug or service problem, include as much of the following as you can:

- The error message or code
- A request ID, if one is available
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened

This helps us investigate faster and get you a useful answer sooner.

## Security Vulnerabilities

If you believe you have found a security issue, please report it privately to [security@terrascale.com](mailto:security@terrascale.com). Please do not post security vulnerabilities in public channels.

## Response Times

- **Community support**: best effort, response times can vary.
- **Email support**: we aim to respond within 24 to 48 hours during business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I reset my password?

Use the password reset option on the sign-in page. If you run into trouble, email [support@terrascale.com](mailto:support@terrascale.com).

### How do I change my plan?

You can review plan options in the [pricing reference](/reference/pricing/) and update your subscription from your account settings.

### How do I report a bug?

Send the details to [support@terrascale.com](mailto:support@terrascale.com), or ask in Discord if you want help confirming whether the behavior is expected.

### How do I request a feature?

Feature requests are welcome in Discord or by email. Sharing your use case helps us understand what would be most useful.
